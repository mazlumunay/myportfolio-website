My Professional Journey

My path to software development began with a strong foundation in Electrical and Electronics Engineering, where I earned both my Bachelor's and Master's degrees from Dokuz Eylul University in Turkey. During my five years as a Software Engineer at Katip Çelebi University, I discovered my passion for developing scalable solutions and integrating new technologies into existing systems. Seeking to deepen my expertise in modern web development, I made the bold decision to move to Canada and pursue a Graduate Certificate in Full Stack Software Development at Lambton College, where I graduated with Dean's Honor List recognition. This transformation allowed me to bridge my engineering background with cutting-edge software development skills, leading to my current role as a Full Stack Developer at Serpo Technology in Toronto. My journey from engineering fundamentals to full-stack development has equipped me with a unique perspective on problem-solving, combining analytical thinking with practical software solutions to create meaningful user experiences.
